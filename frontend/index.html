How to Prepare

  • Create an emergency kit with food, water, and first aid supplies.
  • Establish a family communication plan and designate a meeting point.
  • Know your local evacuation routes and shelter locations.
  • Secure important documents in a waterproof bag.
  • Stay informed by following official alerts and warnings.
